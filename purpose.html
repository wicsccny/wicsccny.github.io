PURPOSE

Membership is open to ALL students at the City College of New York.

Gender Equality

WiCS CCNY aims to bring together women and men who support the advancement of our fellow women in computer science, in academia, and in the industry.

Educate

Education plays a vital role in WiCS. We want to expose everyone from all disciplines to Computer Science, facilitate learning, and work together to solve problems.

Community

Our network connects all CCNY students to an open and diverse community. By doing so, this opens opportunities to build connections both professionally and personally.
